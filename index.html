Welcome to PawPad

PawPad is your pet care command center. It keeps your dogs and cats looked after — coordinating walkers, sitters, vet visits, and feeding schedules, even when you're away or asleep.

Quick tour — about 60 seconds. Swipe or tap Next.

😩

The problem it solves

Your dog walker cancels at 11 PM. You're travelling and get the text at midnight. Who covers? Who do you call? PawPad handles it automatically — your pets are covered before you even wake up.

✈️

Away mode

Turn on Away mode when you leave. The agent watches your pet care schedule and acts autonomously — no waking you up at midnight for a problem it can solve itself.

Example:Walker cancels at 11 PM. Agent texts your backup list. First YES gets the walk. You get a summary at 7 AM. Biscuit's morning walk never missed.
🏠

Dashboard

Your morning view. See what's happening today for each pet, any alerts the agent resolved overnight, and your pets' current status — fed, walked, covered.

📅

Calendar

Color-coded schedule of every visit, walk, sit, vet trip, and grooming appointment across all your pets.

Care roster

Add your walker, pet sitter, and as many backups as you want. They never need to download anything — the agent contacts them by SMS only. First YES gets the job.

🔭

Lookahead & visit log

30-day lookahead flags upcoming vet appointments, grooming due dates, medication reminders, and gaps in care coverage.

Visit log tallies who visited, for how long, and what you owe — every Sunday morning. You pay your carers directly.
